Stage 1 — Initial Posture and Strikes

This page outlines the first stage of US (Trump administration) operations and Iranian responses — a concise, factual timeline of the opening actions and posture.

Phase 0 — Diplomatic Breakdown

Escalating sanctions and failed negotiations lead to heightened tensions. The US places additional military assets in the region and issues public warnings to Iranian leadership.

Day 1 — Targeted Kinetic Options Authorized

The administration authorizes precision strikes against select Iranian nuclear and command-and-control facilities to degrade imminent capabilities identified by intelligence assessments.

Day 3 — Limited Airstrikes

Air assets execute coordinated strikes on multiple declared military sites. Strikes are described as limited and targeted, intended to avoid broader civilian harm while achieving military objectives.

Day 7 — Regional Messaging & Escalation Control

Public statements from the White House emphasize restraint but warn of further action if Iranian proxies or forces retaliate against US personnel or allies.

This summary is a staged overview for educational and planning purposes.
